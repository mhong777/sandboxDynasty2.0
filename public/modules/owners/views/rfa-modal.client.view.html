Rules for RFAs

You have your entire budget to use on RFAs

This includes the cost of all of your keepers as well as the players you are trying to keep in free agency

You are NOT guaranteed to keep these players

The first portion of the draft will be a "Free Agency" period in which people will be able to bid on these players.

At the end of Free Agency you will have the option to match the highest bids and keep players at that price (contract will start at 0 again)

You will NOT have options to do anything with your keepers at the end of Free Agency
